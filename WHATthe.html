2016 - 2022: Catch-up after COVID, Margo brings 1760 Bush back to CCSD

In 2016, a Quorum [a minimum of 15 qualified 18 year old Residents] of Residents attended a General Meeting of 1760 Bush Street and voted to exit CCSD.

In 2022, Margo negotiated with then CCSD President Dennis to bring 1760 back to CCSD and was appointed Interm Secretary by Dennis to finish the 2022 term.

In 2022, Margo reaffirmed 1760 Bush's return to CCSD and committing 1760 Bush's Tech Funds to CCSD. [This is often refered to as a fee, but it is not. HUD mandates a portion of HUD funds be set aside as "Tech Funds" designated for Nominations & Elections and other functionary duties required to operate the Tenant Association democratically.]

In March 2023, Margo was appointed to a CCSD Committee to facilitate the other Affiliated Tenant Associations out of dormancy from the Lockdown by helping Tenants and Boards re-engage, catch up with compliance, prepare for the upcoming 2025 version of the HUD/RAD program, and offer an "Officer Training" to help Tenants understand what the Tenant Association Officers do and hopefully find Tenants interested in serving on a Board.

The Committee appointment came with a six-month probation to ensure "we had a good fit" before appointing Margo the Interm President.

In January 2024, Margo announced she was appointed Interm President of CCSD.
